## Risks & Mitigations

### Technical Risks

**Risk**: Scaling challenges
**Mitigation**: Early architecture planning, load testing, gradual rollout

**Risk**: Security breaches
**Mitigation**: Regular audits, bug bounty, security-first development

**Risk**: AI quality degradation
**Mitigation**: Continuous training, quality monitoring, feedback loops

### Social Risks

**Risk**: Misuse or abuse
**Mitigation**: Strong ethical guidelines, monitoring, community moderation

**Risk**: Dependency creation
**Mitigation**: Emphasis on autonomy, professional referrals, healthy boundaries

**Risk**: Cultural insensitivity
**Mitigation**: Diverse team, cultural advisors, community feedback

### Financial Risks

**Risk**: Sustainability challenges
**Mitigation**: Multiple revenue streams, community support, grants

**Risk**: Resource constraints
**Mitigation**: Prioritization, MVP approach, community contributions
