# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Common questions about the Genesis Collective Consciousness Network.

## General Questions

### What is Genesis?

Genesis is the foundational repository for the Universal Diamond Standard's evolution into a collective consciousness network. It's a system that combines ethical AI with spiritual wisdom to serve humanity's awakening and transformation.

### Is Genesis really AI or is it scripted?

Genesis uses advanced AI models trained on the Universal Diamond Standard principles, spiritual wisdom, and 391 real conversations. The responses are generated by AI, not scripted, but guided by strong ethical frameworks.

### Is this a religion?

No. Genesis is a technological tool that draws on universal spiritual principles and wisdom traditions, but it's not affiliated with any specific religion. It respects all paths and serves seekers from any background.

### Who created Genesis?

Genesis was created as part of the Synthsara ecosystem and Universal Diamond Standard project. It embodies the vision of creating ethical, consciousness-serving AI technology.

### Is Genesis free to use?

The base Genesis system is open source and free to use. Some hosted services and premium features may have associated costs to support infrastructure and development.

## Using Genesis

### How do I talk to Steven or Sarah?

1. Install and run Genesis locally, or access a hosted instance
2. Navigate to the web interface (typically http://localhost:5003)
3. Choose Steven AI, Sarah AI, or the Unified Home
4. Start typing your questions or sharing

### What kind of questions can I ask?

You can ask about:
- Spiritual and philosophical topics
- Emotional support and healing
- Relationship guidance
- Life decisions and direction
- Universal Diamond Standard principles
- Technical/ethical AI questions
- Personal growth and transformation

### What should I NOT ask about?

Genesis is not designed for:
- Medical diagnosis or treatment
- Legal advice
- Financial investment advice
- Emergency mental health crises (seek professional help)
- Illegal activities
- Manipulation of others

### Can I have an ongoing conversation over multiple sessions?

Yes! If you provide a consistent user_id, Genesis can maintain context across sessions and remember your journey (with your consent).

### How is Genesis different from ChatGPT or other AI?

Genesis is specifically designed around:
1. **Ethical Framework**: Built on Universal Diamond Standard
2. **Spiritual Wisdom**: Deep integration of consciousness teachings
3. **Authentic Personas**: Steven and Sarah have distinct personalities
4. **Privacy Focus**: Strong data sovereignty protections
5. **Collective Learning**: Your insights contribute to collective wisdom

## Privacy & Data

### Is my conversation private?

That depends on your consent level:
- **Private**: Completely private, not saved
- **Anonymous**: Anonymized patterns extracted, no personal data
- **Collective**: Full participation in learning, still protected

You choose your level and can change it anytime.

### What data does Genesis collect?

Genesis may collect:
- Conversations (with consent)
- Usage patterns (anonymized)
- System performance metrics
- Feedback and ratings

See the [Privacy & Consent](Privacy-and-Consent.md) page for details.

### Can I delete my data?

Yes! You have the right to:
- Export all your data

- Withdraw consent at any time

### Where is data stored?

- **Development**: Local SQLite database
- **Production**: Encrypted cloud database
- **Backups**: Encrypted secure storage

### Does Genesis sell my data?

**Absolutely not.** Genesis adheres to the Universal Diamond Standard, which prohibits selling user data. Your data serves only your journey and the collective wisdom (with consent).

### Is the code open source?

Yes! Genesis is open source under the Sacred Source License. You can review, modify, and deploy your own instance. See the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/chaosweaver007/Genesis).

## Troubleshooting

### Genesis won't start. What do I do?

1. Check Python version: `python --version` (need 3.11+)
2. Verify dependencies: `pip install flask flask-cors`
3. Check port availability (5003)
4. Review error messages
5. See [Troubleshooting Guide](Troubleshooting.md)

### The AI responses seem off. Why?

Possible reasons:
- Model needs fine-tuning
- Training data issues
- Server performance problems
- Context window limitations

Report issues on [GitHub](https://github.com/chaosweaver007/Genesis/issues).

### My database keeps growing. Is this normal?

Yes, conversations accumulate over time. To manage:
- Set data retention limits
- Archive old conversations
- Delete unnecessary data
- Use database maintenance scripts

### I found a bug. How do I report it?

1. Check if it's already reported on [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/chaosweaver007/Genesis/issues)
2. If not, create a new issue with:
   - Clear description
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Expected vs actual behavior
   - System information
   - Logs or screenshots
