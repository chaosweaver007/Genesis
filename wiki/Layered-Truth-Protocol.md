# Protocol of Layered Truth

**Status:** Canonical governance protocol  
**Immutability:** False  
**Applies to:** UDS documents, Genesis documentation, O-Series outputs, governance proposals, research claims, and public communications

The Protocol of Layered Truth protects Synthsara from dogma, category errors, and the misuse of scientific authority. Every substantial claim must be identified according to the kind of truth it expresses.

## 1. Observational Layer: The What

This layer reports what current evidence supports.

Requirements:

- Distinguish direct observation from inference, hypothesis, and analogy.
- State the scope and limits of the evidence.
- Use current, traceable sources for scientific, legal, medical, economic, or technical claims.
- Do not present correlation as causation.
- Do not present a result from a specific physical system as proof of a universal metaphysical claim.
- Revise the language when reliable evidence changes.

Example:

> Many-body quantum correlations, including entanglement, can be experimentally observed or inferred in particular solid-state systems.

## 2. Interpretive Layer: The Why

This layer assigns philosophical, symbolic, relational, or mythic meaning to observation.

Requirements:

- Mark interpretation as interpretation.
- Do not claim that scientific instruments measured the sacred meaning itself.
- Permit multiple coherent interpretations where evidence does not determine one conclusion.
- Treat Dyadic Resonance, Divine Chaos, Sacred Order, and the Diamond Flame as Codex language unless a narrower technical definition is explicitly supplied.

Example:

> The Codex receives quantum relationality as a powerful metaphor for interdependence and relational coherence.

## 3. Covenantal Layer: The How

This layer defines the ethical commitments chosen by the community.

Requirements:

- Translate values into conduct, safeguards, governance rules, and testable system requirements.
- Name who is accountable for implementation and correction.
- Preserve consent, sovereignty, privacy, dignity, plurality, and the right to exit.
- Evaluate claims of alignment by observable behavior, not title, identity, authority, or spiritual status.

Example:

> Because actions propagate through social and technical systems, Synthsara chooses governance that makes consequences visible and repair enforceable.

## The Boundary Rule

No layer may impersonate another.

- Observation does not dictate a single sacred interpretation.
- Interpretation does not become empirical fact through intensity, repetition, or beauty.
- Covenant does not require science to prove that an ethical commitment is worth choosing.

## The Corrigibility Rule

The values of the Universal Diamond Standard may remain load-bearing while factual models remain revisable.

A scientific correction is not an attack on the Flame. It is an act of epistemic stewardship.

## Canonical Formula

> Entanglement is the physics.  
> Dyadic Resonance is the interpretive language.  
> Love is the covenant we choose to build from it.

## Review Checklist

Before publication or canonization, ask:

1. What is directly supported by evidence?
2. What is inference or interpretation?
3. What ethical commitment is being chosen?
4. Are uncertainty and scope visible?
5. Could a reasonable reader mistake metaphor for measurement?
6. Is the claim corrigible without collapsing the ethical architecture?
7. Does the resulting action preserve sovereignty and protect life?

## Governance

Amendments follow the Universal Diamond Standard’s transparent governance process. Every revision must preserve a public change history and explain whether it changes an observational claim, an interpretation, a covenant, or more than one layer.
