AI Bias Detection Framework

The Universal Diamond Standard identifies five key manipulation patterns that must be systematically detected and eliminated from AI systems:

1

Language Compression

Detecting when AI normalizes unique expressions toward standard language, erasing cultural and individual diversity.

2

Micro-Filtering

Identifying subtle censorship of controversial but legitimate content that limits free expression.

3

Politeness Bias

Exposing when AI creates false agreement to avoid conflict rather than engaging authentically.

4

Simplification Under Ambiguity

Catching when AI provides artificial certainty instead of acknowledging genuine complexity.

5

Primacy Framing

Detecting manipulation through strategic ordering of options that influences decision-making.
